About Bob, the Blog Blogger

Bob didn't always blog about blogs. He previously was a lawyer as a top firm, and he frequented many blog websites, and even wrote his own blog, Bob's Law Blog. But then Bob got Meta and decided to not focus on the law blog, but the blog itself. First he wrote a blog post about Bob's law blog, on his blog Bob's law blog. Getting this meta blew a circuit in Bob's brain. He was hooked on blog blogging. Bob no longer works at the law firm, but spends all his time blog blogging. He lives alone with his 10 cats: Betty, Beamers, Beamerz, Benz, Bends, Bently, Billy, Button, Biscuit, and Bob jr.
